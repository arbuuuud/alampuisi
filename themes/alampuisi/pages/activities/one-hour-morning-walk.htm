One Hour Morning Walk
 

Experience the poetry of the Balinese landscape with a complimentary morning walk through the rice fields. The gentle sway of these lush green fields will soothe your soul, and the fresh morning air will cleanse you body. Your guide will explain the traditions of growing rice, and the cultural importance of this practice to the Balinese.
