Balinese Experiences of Cooking Lesson
 

Learn the secrets of Balinese cuisine with a special lesson from the Anubhooti restaurant chef. The lesson begins with the traditional Balinese morning ritual of visiting the markets to buy the freshest local produce. Our chef will then guide you through the process of making foods with love. You will be finished in time to enjoy your meal for lunch.
